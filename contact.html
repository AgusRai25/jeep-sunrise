Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our jeep tours

What should I bring for the tour?

We recommend bringing comfortable clothing, closed-toe shoes, sunscreen, a hat, water bottle, and your camera. We provide all safety equipment.

What is the best time for sunrise tours?

Sunrise tours typically start around 4:00 AM to ensure we reach the viewpoint before sunrise. The exact time varies by season.

Are the tours suitable for children?

Yes! Our tours are family-friendly. Children under 5 years old can join for free, and we have special safety measures for young adventurers.

What happens if it rains?

We monitor weather conditions closely. If conditions are unsafe, we'll reschedule your tour or provide a full refund. Light rain doesn't usually affect our tours.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance, especially during peak season. Last-minute bookings are possible if availability allows.

Do you provide hotel pickup?

Yes! We offer hotel pickup and drop-off for most areas in Bali. Additional charges may apply for locations outside our standard pickup zone.
