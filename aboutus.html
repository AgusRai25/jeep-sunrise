About Sunrise Jeep

Discover our story, mission, and the team behind your adventures

Our Story

Born from a passion for adventure and a love for the mountains, Sunrise Jeep began as a dream to share the breathtaking beauty of Bali's highlands with the world.

Founded in 2019, we started with just one jeep and a vision to create unforgettable off-road experiences. What began as a small family operation has grown into one of Bali's most trusted adventure companies, serving thousands of happy customers from around the globe.

Our journey started in the picturesque village of Kintamani, where we discovered the most stunning sunrise viewpoints and challenging mountain trails. We realized that these hidden gems deserved to be shared with adventure seekers who crave authentic experiences.
